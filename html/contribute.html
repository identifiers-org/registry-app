Communicate

Talk to the team

The team developing and maintaining the Registry can be contacted by email via: biomodels-net-support AT lists.sf.net

You can consult the archives of this mailing list but cannot subscribe to it.

Bug reports and feature requests

Please use the relevant tickets on our SourceForge project:

  • bug reports
  • feature requests

General (community wide) discussions

One mailing list for general discussions about BioModels.net projects (which includes the Registry) is available via: biomodels-net-discuss AT lists.sf.net

Please, feel free to subscribe to it and consult the archives.

Request new data collection(s)

To request new data collection(s), please use the relevant ticket on our SourceForge project.

Update existing data collection(s)

If you find any obsolete or incorrect information in the Registry, please follow the "Suggest modifications" link that you can find at the bottom of each page. Thank you.
