About the Registry

The MIRIAM Registry is developed by the BioModels.net team (part of the PST at EMBL-EBI, UK), in collaboration with the Le Novère lab (Babraham Institute, UK).

Acknowledgements

The MIRIAM Registry is currently funded by the Biotechnology and Biological Sciences Research Council, the European Molecular Biology Laboratory and Open PHACTS.

Former funders

The MIRIAM Registry also benefited from funding from ELIXIR and the National Institute of General Medical Sciences.
