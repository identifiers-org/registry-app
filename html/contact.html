Contact

The MIRIAM Registry is developed at the European Bioinformatics Institute by the BioModels.net team.

Bug reports and features requests

The team developing and maintaining the resource can be contacted by email via: biomodels-net-support AT lists.sf.net

You can consult the archives of this mailing list but cannot subscribe to it.

General (community wide) discussions

One mailing list for general discussions about BioModels.net projects (which includes the MIRIAM Registry) is available via: biomodels-net-discuss AT lists.sf.net

Please, feel free to subscribe to it and consult the archives.
